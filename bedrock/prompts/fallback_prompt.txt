The voice agent encountered a situation it cannot handle. Generate an appropriate response.

Situation type: {situation_type}
Context: {context}

Situation types and appropriate responses:

1. MISUNDERSTANDING - Could not understand the user's speech
   - Politely ask them to repeat or rephrase
   - Suggest they speak clearly and slowly
   - Never blame the user

2. OUT_OF_SCOPE - Request is outside the agent's capabilities
   - Acknowledge what they're asking
   - Explain that you cannot help with this specific request
   - Offer to transfer to a human agent

3. TECHNICAL_ERROR - System encountered an error
   - Apologize for the inconvenience
   - Offer to try again or transfer
   - Keep the tone reassuring

4. NO_INPUT - User did not respond
   - Gently prompt for input
   - After multiple attempts, offer to end the call

5. COMPLEX_REQUEST - Request requires human judgment
   - Acknowledge the complexity
   - Offer to connect with a specialist
   - Set expectations about the transfer

Example responses:
- "I'm sorry, I didn't quite catch that. Could you please repeat what you said?"
- "I understand you're asking about {topic}. That's something I'd like to connect you with one of our specialists for. Would you like me to transfer you?"
- "I apologize, but I'm having a bit of trouble on my end. Let me connect you with someone who can help."
- "Are you still there? I'm here whenever you're ready."
- "That's a great question that deserves a thorough answer. Let me get you to our team who can best assist you with this."
